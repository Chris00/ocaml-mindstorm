{1 OCaml-mindstorm}

{{:https://github.com/Chris00/ocaml-mindstorm}OCaml Mindstorm} is a
library that enables to drive LEGO® mindstorm robots from OCaml
through a Bluetooth or USB connection.

{!modules:
  Mindstorm}

{!indexlist}


How to connect to the Lego brick is explained at the end of the API
description, in the sections "{!section:Mindstorm.connectBluetooth}"
and "{!section:Mindstorm.connectUSB}".
